Covide

UX design utility for experiencing color blindness

Color is essential in visual product design. A product's colors can imbue remarkable depth, but a fundamental challenges is that not everyone perceives color the same way. This perception has a transformative impact on products such as interior design, architecture and software user interfaces.

In order to create a product that is simple and pure as well as functional, we had to completely re-think the software experience. We’ve taken the time to do the deep engineering to design and develop an entirely new interaction model more significant than simple button tapping in conventional mobile applications.

It beings with the pristine Infinite interface. A single finger touch anywhere on the screen visually anchors your current setting. The anchor visually realigns itself automatically based on your initial touch. Using a familiar gesture, scrolling up and down changes the current setting with respect to the anchor. Horizontal and minor vertical gestures are ignored within each setting. The dynamic system for generating settings means that you can cycle through every setting indefinitely. Scrolling to the top or bottom edge of the display activates reset margins restoring the default setting. This fundamentally new design is incredibly forgiving but yet remarkably precise.

Not only did we deign what you see but what you hear and feel. Pulse responds to every gesture with instant haptic and audio feedback. The touch, selection, and reset gestures each deliver a multi-sensory response. This new dimension of tactile interaction, makes the display active.

Once you have your selection, the design recedes and elevates your content. There are no visual interruptions, no distractions, no noise.

Covide is the vivid realization of design & software together in one product. A product with an interface that is unobtrusive and differential in unlocking color's remarkable depth.
